## Post 7 — Developer / Hacker News (Hacker News / Dev.to)

> **Show HN: Aurora Rayes ComplianceOS — We built the compliance platform we always wanted**
>
> Hey HN — we're a team of engineers who spent way too many hours filling out SOC 2 spreadsheets and decided to build something better.
>
> The stack: Next.js 14 (App Router) + Supabase (Postgres + Auth + RLS) + Stripe + OpenAI GPT-4 + Vercel.
>
> The product automates compliance workflows for SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, and PCI-DSS. The AI gap analysis runs your control matrix against framework requirements and produces a prioritised remediation plan.
>
> Architecture decisions we made:
> - RLS everywhere — complete tenant isolation at the DB level, not just the app layer
> - Edge middleware for auth to keep latency low
> - Sliding window rate limiting without Redis (in-memory, restarts fine on Edge)
> - Zod for all API input validation
>
> Code is available at github.com/21leahcimhtiek-oss/complianceos
>
> Happy to answer questions about the architecture or the compliance domain.
