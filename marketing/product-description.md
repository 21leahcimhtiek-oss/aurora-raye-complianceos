# Aurora Rayes ComplianceOS — Product Description

## Stop drowning in compliance spreadsheets.

Aurora Rayes ComplianceOS is the all-in-one compliance automation platform built for engineering-led teams. Whether you are preparing for your first SOC 2 audit, maintaining ISO 27001 certification, or navigating HIPAA, GDPR, and PCI-DSS simultaneously — Aurora Rayes ComplianceOS replaces your messy spreadsheets, shared Google Docs, and consultant invoices with a single, audit-ready platform.

---

## The Problem

Compliance is broken for most startups and growth-stage companies:

- **Spreadsheets** that fall out of date the moment someone closes the tab
- **Audits that cost $50,000+** and take 6 months to prepare for
- **Evidence collection** that requires bugging engineers every week
- **No visibility** into your compliance posture until the auditor arrives
- **Consultants** who charge $300/hour to tell you things you already know

The result? Companies fail audits, lose enterprise deals, and spend millions on compliance theatre.

---

## The Solution

Aurora Rayes ComplianceOS automates the boring parts of compliance so your team can focus on building:

### 🤖 AI Gap Analysis
Our GPT-4-powered gap analysis scans your controls, cross-references industry benchmarks, and produces a prioritised remediation plan in minutes — not months.

### 📋 Compliance Frameworks
SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, and PCI-DSS — all in one place. Add a framework and get an instant control matrix with evidence requirements, ownership assignments, and completion tracking.

### 🔍 Findings Tracker
Never lose track of a compliance gap. Create findings with severity levels (Critical/High/Medium/Low), assign them to team members, set due dates, and track resolution from open to closed.

### 📅 Audit Scheduler
Plan and manage your audit calendar. Track upcoming audits, coordinate with auditors, and generate audit-ready evidence packs with one click.

### 📊 Compliance Reports
Generate board-level executive summaries, detailed gap analysis reports, and framework-specific compliance matrices in PDF or CSV format — on demand or on schedule.

### 👥 Team Collaboration
Invite your security team, engineers, and legal counsel. Assign controls and findings to the right people. Everyone knows exactly what they need to do.
